Top Boy - Make your own TV Commercial Campaign (2013)

This is a site built for Channel 4 (UK) to promote the release of season 2 of the TV drama 'Top Boy'.
This site has a video editing tool embedded within it, which allows users to create their own Top Boy TV commercial using audio and video snippets from the show. The site would then upload these videos to the official Top Boy Youtube channel. Most liked video would then be aired on TV.
I worked on both the front and back end of this site.
