Prostate Cancer UK Men United (menunited.org)

This is a microsite built for Prostate Cancer Uk's men united campaign. This site was used along side a frequently TV and print advertisements in the UK. Because of this the site needed to handle high traffic and frequent spikes.
I worked on both the front and back end side of this site.

Prostate Cancer UK Main site (prostatecanceruk.org)

This is the main site for the Prostate Cancer UK organisation, which is one of the largest cancer charities in the UK.
The site is content heavy which is managed using Umbraco CMS.
I was responsible for managing the migration of the site and it's infrastructure to a new hosting environment on Amazon AWS.
