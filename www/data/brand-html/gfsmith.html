G F Smith main site - (gfsmith.com)

G F Smith are a leading supplier of paper to the creative industry.
GFSmith.com is an E-commerce site used to promote and sell their products. It has a bespoke frontend design to meet the complex branding requirements of a high-end design company like G F Smith.

This site helped G F Smith win a Black Pencil at the acclaimed D&AD awards.

I worked on both the front and backend areas of this site.
