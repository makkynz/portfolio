Westpac Hotpoints (www.hotpoints.co.nz)

Hotpoints is Westpac's credit card loyalty programme. I worked on the front and back end developments of the loyalty points redemption website (hotpoints.co.nz). The backend system consisted of a complex points calculation engine that would process hundreds of thousands of credit transactions daily. The backend also required to support the e-commerce functions of the points redemption site (e.g catalogue and order managment, payment gateways).
I worked on hotpoints whilst in my role as senior analyst programmer at Aimia Ltd.
