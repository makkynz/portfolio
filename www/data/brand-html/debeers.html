Debeers Forevermark Register My Diamond (forevermark.com)

This site allows Forevermark diamond owners to register their diamond pieces on the official Debeers forevermark.com site. Once registered, diamond owners can view further information about their diamond, enter promotions and read blog posts written by diamond experts.

The site is localised and multi-lingual for use across Debeer's major markets. e.g Japan, China, UK, US, India.

I worked on both the front and backend coding pieces of this site.
